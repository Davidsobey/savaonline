SAVA FAQ

Get The Info You Need

How Much Is A SAVA Suit?

A suit that is made for you will depend on the fabric that you choose and also whether you are in the market for a 3 or 2 piece suit.

How Long Does It Take To Make A Bespoke Suit

A bespoke suit can be made in roughly 1 week however the time will depend on the fittings and how well the fittings go. A suit completed with fitting may take up to 3 weeks.

Do You Have Any Suits For Sales?

Unfortunately, we do not stock any off the rack suits. We make all our suits to perfection and you get a say in how you want them every step of the way.

Do You Offer An Alteration Service?

Yes, we definitely do offer alterations for suits that are made by us. You get 3 alterations from us for free upon the purchase of a bespoke suit.

Do You Lease Out Suits?

No we do not offer a suit rental service. Each suit we make is made to the specific measurements of the customer and their personal preferences.

Dry Cleaning

We offer dry cleaning services for all SAVA suits. Dry cleaning is the best way to maintain your suit.
