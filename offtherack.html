Off The Rack vs Bespoke vs Made to Measure

There is a difference between custom suits and bespoke suits.
A bespoke suit is made from scratch for you and only you , bespoke is more expensive due to the time spent on each suit. Each suit is hand crafted, measured to perfection with specific tools. There is no fabric cut until you have been measured to create your suit. When you first put your suit on though, it isn’t perfect because tailors leave some room in order to make the suit fit like a glove It's custom made to perfection to your liking from choosing your own color, texture, lining and material.
You get to determine where your pockets should go, how many buttons you desire It's made for you and nobody else can wear it.
Advantages of this:
You save a lot of time instead of going to a shop looking for one then finding the perfect color, size. And we all like to feel special in ourselves so now that you know something is yours made by you and to your liking, no one can tell you otherwise. Made to measure suits are suits where fabric and patterns have already been cut however they are customized to your personal preference and customized to your measurements Off the rack suits , is when you go to a store, find your size and then possibly do alterations to try suit your body type . It can be uncomfortable to wear as certain parts tend to pull. An off the rack suit can be expensive or cheap depending on the suit that you purchase although these are suits generally made with machines. If you purchase an off the rack suit from a store and get it tailored it isn’t a bespoke suit , it’s a tailored suit.
