WHAT IS A HANDKERCHIEF?

A Handkerchief or hanky also known as a pocket square, is a thin piece of fabric sewed together intended for the use of personal hygiene. The term is derived from two french words known as couvrir which means to cover and chef which means head.
What can a Handkerchief be used for? A Handkerchief is a multipurpose fabric that can be used to mop your brow, clean your glasses, blow your nose, dry your hands, offer to a crying lady, headband or head covering, a bandage. And also well it just looks good and makes you look like a gentleman Make sure that you always carry a clean Handkerchief and that it isn't used when you offer it to somebody else In my opinion, I think that only a true gentleman carries a Handkerchief.
However I don't believe that a gentleman to use his Handkerchief to blow his Nose into but rather use it for the courtesy of others.
