TIE OR BOW TIE?

Well this will depend on the occasion and the type of suit you’re wearing. Bow ties are generally more formal and used when a tuxedo is worn. In the modern day, there has been more creativity in bow ties and people tend to wear them whenever they find suitable depending on the design. Long ties are more common when wearing a suit.
If your suit is double breasted or single button blazer go with a bow tie however if it’s a two or three button blazer go with a tie unless it’s a formal event. What to wear with what shirt? If you’re wearing a solid colour shirt you should go with a patterned tie and the opposites applies too if you want something very straight forward, wear solid on solid but don’t go for the same colour. Throw in something that compliments the shirt you wear. Sometimes, if you’re trying to go for the cool but Klassy look, just leave the shirt unbuttoned without the hassle of choosing a tie or bow tie.
